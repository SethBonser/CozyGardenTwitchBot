🌸

How It Works

  1. Get a seed Redeem the Get a Seed channel point reward (or use !buyseed in petals mode) to receive a random seed in your hand.
  2. Plant it Type !plant to drop your seed into the first empty garden slot, or !plant 2 to choose a specific slot.
  3. Water & grow You and other viewers water plants with !water. Each stage (Sprout → Budding → Bloom) needs a set number of waters.
  4. Harvest! Once a plant blooms, type !harvest to collect petals. Part of the payout is shared with everyone active in chat.
